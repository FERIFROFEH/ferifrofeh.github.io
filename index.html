About Fariba Mehmandoost

My Childhood passed by playing with fabric.
I always loved to create a modern artwork with
a few facilities I had and created my own new
sewing by looking at the delicacies of the
clothes sewing.


I loved delicacy and handiwork, and enjoyed
the sound of scissor and Sewing Machine,
In my whole life I was learning and hustling.


Love flows between my fingers, I believe
that this fine art is a gift from the god
to me, what I’m very thankful for,
and day and night, I kiss my hands,
I ll do my best for promoting this
industry.
